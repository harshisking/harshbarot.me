O Nature

Last Updated: 13/01/2026 • Status: Complete

O mother nature,
forgive us , your children,
With gifts unmatched,
We tried to bend you, the caring mother.

We have cut the trees,
your beautiful hair,
to make our lairs,
and finally now we cease.

O mother, forgive us
your children for the pain,
for we are just kids in your lap,
awaiting your pat of forgiveness.

O children of earth,
arise for earth,
which has sheltered you so long,
arise for nature, the caring mother.
